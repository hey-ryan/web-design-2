Singh's Vietnamese Covid-19 Response

We are monitoring closely the impact of the coronavirus (covid-19) outbreak and we are taking steps to ensure the continuity of our service.

We have implemented rigorous protocols to protect our community and our employees. The well-being and safety of our team and community is very important to us. We will begin to operate under a new schedule with our same menu as supply allows.

In order to help prevent the spread of covid-19 we have implemented the following measures:

  • We suspended dine-in. WE will continue to provide pick-up and delivery only. Customers will still be able to order via our square store and in person.
  • We have introduced curb side service for pick up orders.
  • We have implemented a new cleaning and sanitization protocol of all of our surfaces every 45 minutes during the course of service.

We appreciate your understanding and patience as we continue to deal with these unprecedented times.

We will keep you updated via our website and social media channels and we will make decisions that ensure the health and safety of our community and our staff.
